The Evening Awaits

⸸

You are cordially summoned to an evening of crimson and shadow. Behind ornate masks and red velvet curtains, the ancient ones gather for a feast — course by course, secret by secret, blood by blood.

But even among the immortal, trust is a luxury. Before the final glass is drained, one among you will fall. The rest must unravel the web of lies — or let the killer disappear into the fog.

Dine. Deceive. Devour. Discover.
