The Evening Awaits

⸸

You are cordially summoned to an evening of crimson and shadow.

Behind ornate masks and red velvet curtains, the ancient ones gather for a feast — course by course, secret by secret, blood by blood. But even among the immortal, trust is a luxury.

Before the final glass is drained, one among you will fall. The rest must unravel the web of lies — or let the killer disappear into the fog.

Dine · Deceive · Devour · Discover

The Evening Unfolds

🕯

The night has five acts. Each course served brings new secrets to light.

7:00 PM
I

The Gathering

Guests arrive in costume and mask. Sealed envelopes are distributed — your character, your secrets, your motives. Cocktails flow. The ancient ones size each other up.

8:00 PM
II

Blood is Served

The feast begins. As the first course is placed, a body is discovered. The evening's true purpose becomes clear. Alliances form. Stories align — or don't.

8:45 PM
III

The Web Tightens

New evidence surfaces with the second course. Motives sharpen. Accusations begin. Someone is lying — and someone else knows it.

9:30 PM
IV

The Trial

Dessert and reckoning arrive together. The Houses convene. Each guest submits their accusation. Alliances are broken. Masks — literal and otherwise — begin to slip.

10:30 PM
V

The Unmasking

The killer is revealed. The truth laid bare. Whoever solved the mystery wins eternal glory — and a very gothic prize. The night isn't over; it just gets darker.
